Welcome to GSW Foundation

We provide Social Services for the Common Good.
We support in creating Interdependence for Existence and Growth.
We are based out of Ghaziabad in National Capital Region of India.


Sardaar Jagdish Singh Rana Goodwill & Social Welfare Foundation Is About The Well-Being Of The Entire Society, And It Is Not Same As The Standard Of Living But It Is More Related With The Quality Of Life, That Too At The Global Level While Encouraging The Concept Of Interdependence For Existence And Growth. And To Improve The Quality Of Life At Every Step, Expanse Of Education Opportunities, Spread Of Career Options, Imbibing The Culture Of Work Life Balance, Strengthening The Fabric Of Society, Sustenance Of The Family Structure, Empowerment Of The Women, Aiding The Agrarian Culture, Are Few Of The Ways. Therefore, An Endeavour To Create Such Possibilities And To Spread Education & Career Choices, Women Empowerment And Agrarian Support In Any Form Or Shape, Amongst Individuals Or Groups, Towards Providing Strong Support To Build A Conscientious Global Society, An Intent Took A Shape Of An Idea In The Form Of SJSR Goodwill & Social Welfare Foundation.

OUR INSPIRATION

Sardaar Jagdish Singh Rana

26th September 1943 - 12th June 2021

He led a beautiful life. He was an Obedient Son, a Dedicated Husband, a Wonderful Father and a Graceful Grandfather.
He touched many lives with his presence which was full of love, care and affection. He was a Warrior of Life. An achiever beyond imagination, despite the hurdles he came across. Professionally, he was an Agriculturist and remained a farmerâ€™s son in every endeavour of his life.
He knew the worth of sowing the seeds of good deeds, protecting his yield and sharing his produce with the worthy. He never ever hesitated in extending a helping hand to the needy and never expected anything in return. He remained a God Loving Man throughout his life.
He is and will always be remembered for his saintly nature, by one and all, forever. Sardaar Jagdish Singh Rana Goodwill And Social Welfare Foundation is a tribute to him in his remembrance.

our Projects

The Educationist Project by GSW Foundation

a. The purpose of this project is the welfare of the society through Education, Employability, Entrepreneurship, Career Advisory, Counselling & Management.
b. To sponsor, promote, develop aid and run institutions, trusts, societies, schools, teacher training institutes, educational institutions, libraries and other charitable societies.
c. To promote entrepreneurial skills to the unemployed youth and creation of self-employment opportunities for them.
d. To establish and run institutes which shall be non-profit organizations devoted to education.
e. To provide scholarships and special facilities to financially deprived students irrespective of their religion, race, caste, creed, gender and place of birth or any of them.
f. To organize cultural shows, dramas, fetes and sports for the benefit of the society on non-commercial basis.

The Red Dot Project by GSW Foundation

The Red Dot Project by Goodwill & Social Welfare Foundation is a social service project, of the women, for the women and by the women, in the areas of creating awareness about the importance of Mental, Physical and Emotional Health of Women, and by aligning corporate / Institutional collaborations of different types, they are creating economic empowerment to strengthen the Financial Health of women as well .

The Agrarian Project by GSW Foundation

To help, support and promote organic agrarian way of life.
To create an environment where organic products are endorsed, embraced and adopted.
To bring all the agribusiness professionals on one platform as a Consortium of all the agricultural and allied products and services.
